# Best Flooring Options for Calgary Homes: Complete 2024 Guide

Choosing the right flooring for your Calgary home is crucial. Calgary's climate, with its cold winters and dry conditions, requires flooring that can handle temperature fluctuations and provide comfort year-round.

## Top Flooring Options for Calgary Homes

### 1. Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP) - Most Popular in Calgary

**Why Calgary Homeowners Love LVP:**
- **Waterproof**: Perfect for Calgary's variable climate
- **Durable**: Handles temperature changes well
- **Affordable**: Great value for money
- **Easy Maintenance**: Low maintenance requirements
- **Warm Underfoot**: More comfortable than tile in winter

**Best For:**
- Kitchens and bathrooms
- Basements
- High-traffic areas
- Pet owners
- Families with children

**Cost**: $3 - $8 per square foot installed

**Lifespan**: 15-25 years with proper care

### 2. Tile Flooring - Durable & Timeless

**Why Tile Works in Calgary:**
- **Extremely Durable**: Lasts decades
- **Waterproof**: Perfect for wet areas
- **Easy to Clean**: Low maintenance
- **Cool in Summer**: Comfortable during hot spells

**Best For:**
- Bathrooms
- Kitchens
- Entryways
- Laundry rooms

**Types Popular in Calgary:**
- **Ceramic Tile**: $4 - $10/sq ft
- **Porcelain Tile**: $5 - $15/sq ft
- **Large Format Porcelain**: $8 - $20/sq ft (very popular)

**Lifespan**: 30+ years

### 3. Hardwood Flooring - Classic Calgary Choice

**Why Hardwood is Popular:**
- **Timeless Beauty**: Never goes out of style
- **Increases Home Value**: Excellent ROI
- **Durable**: Can last 100+ years with refinishing
- **Warm & Inviting**: Perfect for Calgary's long winters

**Best For:**
- Living rooms
- Dining rooms
- Bedrooms
- Main floor areas

**Types:**
- **Engineered Hardwood**: $6 - $12/sq ft (better for Calgary's climate)
- **Solid Hardwood**: $8 - $15/sq ft
- **Prefinished**: More popular for convenience

**Lifespan**: 50-100+ years with proper care

### 4. Carpet - Comfort for Calgary Winters

**Why Carpet is Great:**
- **Warm & Cozy**: Perfect for cold Calgary winters
- **Sound Dampening**: Reduces noise between floors
- **Comfortable**: Soft underfoot
- **Affordable**: Budget-friendly option

**Best For:**
- Bedrooms
- Family rooms
- Stairs
- Basements (with proper moisture control)

**Types:**
- **Berber**: $3 - $6/sq ft
- **Plush**: $4 - $8/sq ft
- **Commercial Grade**: $5 - $10/sq ft (great for high traffic)

**Lifespan**: 10-15 years typically

### 5. Marmoleum - Eco-Friendly Option

**Why Consider Marmoleum:**
- **Eco-Friendly**: Made from natural materials
- **Antimicrobial**: Naturally resists bacteria
- **Durable**: Long-lasting
- **Unique Look**: Distinctive appearance

**Best For:**
- Kitchens
- Bathrooms
- Eco-conscious homeowners

**Cost**: $5 - $10/sq ft

## Calgary Climate Considerations

### Temperature Fluctuations
Calgary experiences significant temperature changes. Choose flooring that:
- Expands and contracts without damage
- Handles dry winter air
- Works with heating systems

### Humidity Levels
Calgary's dry climate means:
- Hardwood needs proper acclimation
- Some materials may shrink
- Proper installation is crucial

### Winter Considerations
- Warm flooring options (LVP, carpet) are popular
- Tile can be cold but works with radiant heating
- Proper insulation underneath matters

## Room-by-Room Flooring Guide for Calgary Homes

### Kitchen
**Best Options:**
1. Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP) - Most popular
2. Tile - Durable and waterproof
3. Hardwood - Classic choice

**Avoid:** Carpet (not practical for kitchens)

### Bathroom
**Best Options:**
1. Tile - Most common and practical
2. LVP - Waterproof option
3. Marmoleum - Eco-friendly choice

**Avoid:** Hardwood, carpet (moisture issues)

### Basement
**Best Options:**
1. LVP - Waterproof and warm
2. Tile - If properly waterproofed
3. Carpet - With moisture barrier

**Avoid:** Solid hardwood (moisture concerns)

### Living Room
**Best Options:**
1. Hardwood - Classic and valuable
2. LVP - Affordable alternative
3. Carpet - Cozy option

### Bedroom
**Best Options:**
1. Carpet - Cozy and quiet
2. Hardwood - Timeless
3. LVP - Practical choice

## Installation Matters in Calgary

### Why Professional Installation is Crucial:
- **Proper Acclimation**: Materials need to adjust to Calgary's climate
- **Subfloor Preparation**: Critical for longevity
- **Expansion Gaps**: Necessary for temperature changes
- **Moisture Barriers**: Important in basements
- **Warranty**: Professional installation often includes warranty

### Red Flags to Avoid:
- Installation without proper subfloor prep
- No expansion gaps
- Materials not acclimated
- Unlicensed installers

## Maintenance Tips for Calgary Flooring

### LVP
- Regular sweeping and mopping
- Avoid excessive water
- Use felt pads on furniture

### Tile
- Regular cleaning with appropriate cleaners
- Grout sealing recommended
- Avoid harsh chemicals

### Hardwood
- Regular cleaning with wood-specific products
- Refinish every 10-15 years
- Control humidity levels
- Use area rugs in high-traffic areas

### Carpet
- Regular vacuuming
- Professional cleaning annually
- Address spills immediately
- Use carpet protectors
