# Kitchen Renovation Costs in Calgary: Complete Guide 2024

Planning a kitchen renovation in Calgary? Understanding the costs involved is crucial for budgeting your project. As a 3rd generation construction company serving Calgary since 1997, we've completed hundreds of kitchen renovations and understand the local market pricing.

## Average Kitchen Renovation Costs in Calgary

Kitchen renovation costs in Calgary typically range from **$25,000 to $75,000** for a complete renovation, depending on size, materials, and scope of work. Here's a breakdown:

### Small Kitchen Renovation (100-150 sq ft)
- **Basic Renovation**: $25,000 - $40,000
- Includes: New cabinets, countertops, flooring, basic appliances
- Timeline: 4-6 weeks

### Medium Kitchen Renovation (150-200 sq ft)
- **Standard Renovation**: $40,000 - $60,000
- Includes: Custom cabinets, premium countertops, upgraded appliances, lighting
- Timeline: 6-8 weeks

### Large Kitchen Renovation (200+ sq ft)
- **Luxury Renovation**: $60,000 - $100,000+
- Includes: High-end custom cabinets, premium materials, professional-grade appliances
- Timeline: 8-12 weeks

## Factors Affecting Kitchen Renovation Costs in Calgary

### 1. **Cabinets & Millwork**
Custom cabinets are one of the biggest expenses, typically 30-40% of your budget. Options range from:
- Stock cabinets: $5,000 - $15,000
- Semi-custom cabinets: $15,000 - $30,000
- Full custom cabinets: $30,000 - $60,000+

### 2. **Countertops**
Calgary homeowners love quartz, granite, and porcelain slab countertops:
- Laminate: $1,500 - $3,000
- Quartz: $3,000 - $8,000
- Granite: $3,500 - $9,000
- Porcelain Slab: $4,000 - $10,000

### 3. **Appliances**
Quality appliances significantly impact your budget:
- Basic package: $3,000 - $6,000
- Mid-range package: $6,000 - $12,000
- High-end package: $12,000 - $25,000+

### 4. **Flooring**
Popular options in Calgary kitchens:
- Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP): $3,000 - $6,000
- Tile: $4,000 - $8,000
- Hardwood: $5,000 - $10,000

### 5. **Plumbing & Electrical**
Updating plumbing and electrical systems:
- Basic updates: $2,000 - $5,000
- Complete rewiring/replumbing: $5,000 - $12,000

## Calgary-Specific Considerations

### Building Permits
Calgary requires permits for structural changes, electrical, and plumbing work. Budget $500 - $2,000 for permits and inspections.

### Climate Considerations
Calgary's climate means considering:
- Proper insulation for exterior walls
- Quality windows and doors
- Moisture-resistant materials

### Local Material Costs
Material costs in Calgary can vary seasonally. Working with a local contractor ensures you get the best pricing and availability.

## How to Save on Kitchen Renovation Costs

1. **Plan Thoroughly**: Changes mid-project cost significantly more
2. **Reuse What You Can**: Keep existing layout when possible
3. **Choose Mid-Range Materials**: Balance quality and cost
4. **Work with Local Contractors**: Better pricing and knowledge of Calgary market
5. **Time Your Renovation**: Off-season may offer better pricing
