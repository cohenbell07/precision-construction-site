# PCND 30-Day Lead Sprint

## Target

Generate 100 real quote/contact opportunities in 30 days from May 21 to June 20, 2026, with little or no ad spend.

## Lead Definition

A lead is a homeowner, property manager, realtor, landlord, or business owner who gives PCND at least a name plus one contact method and mentions a real project or possible project.

## Daily Scorecard

Track these daily:

- New quote form submissions
- Phone calls from website
- Facebook or Instagram DMs
- Google Business Profile messages/calls
- Referral introductions
- Follow-ups sent
- Reviews requested
- Reviews received

## Lead Math

To reach 100 leads in 30 days:

- 30 leads from past customers and referrals
- 25 leads from Facebook groups, Marketplace, Kijiji, and community posts
- 20 leads from Google Business Profile activity and local search
- 15 leads from partner outreach
- 10 leads from website forms, chat, and price-beat requests

## Week 1: Fix and Activate

1. Post the Spring Build offer on Google Business Profile.
2. Upload 20 project photos to Google Business Profile.
3. Ask every happy past customer for a review.
4. Ask every happy past customer who else they know planning a renovation.
5. Post one helpful project-specific offer per day in local community channels.
6. Contact 10 realtors, property managers, home inspectors, mortgage brokers, designers, and flooring/cabinet suppliers per day.
7. Reply to every lead within 15 minutes during working hours.

## Week 2: Local Proof Push

1. Publish one before/after or project-story post daily.
2. Rotate services: basements, bathrooms, kitchens, flooring, cabinets, showers, decks/fences.
3. Repost the best testimonial with a quote CTA.
4. Follow up with all unclosed leads from Week 1.
5. Ask partners for one warm introduction each.

## Week 3: Offer and Referral Sprint

1. Push "free 24-hour quote" and "5% price beat" together.
2. Ask every quote lead to send photos before the estimate.
3. Post "contractor red flags" and "renovation budget mistakes" educational content.
4. Send the referral script to all past customers again with a softer note.
5. Call the highest-intent leads from the first two weeks.

## Week 4: Close the Loop

1. Repost the strongest offer daily with different project photos.
2. Follow up with everyone who engaged but did not book.
3. Send a "last chance for current promo pricing" message three days before the promo deadline.
4. Ask all new satisfied customers for reviews immediately after walkthrough.
5. Record which channel produced the most serious leads and repeat it.

## Non-Negotiables

- Every post needs one clear action: call, message, or request a free quote.
- Every response should ask for project type, location, timeline, and photos.
- Every serious lead gets same-day follow-up.
- Every completed job or happy client gets a review request.
- Do not argue price in public comments. Invite them to send photos or request a quote.
